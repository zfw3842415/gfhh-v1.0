Building Authentic Relationships through Effective Storytelling

We craft content and stories that connect, move and persuade target audiences. After understanding your business, brand, reputation and goals, we map the universe of influencers that propel or hold back your progress so that we can engage the right people in compelling, culturally relevant and sharable ways both in the digital and traditional realms.

 

Ogilvy Public Relations (Ogilvy PR) is a global, multi-disciplinary communications leader operating in more than 85 offices across six continents. In 2015, Ogilvy PR was crowned Asia Pacific Regional Consultancy of the Year at the SABRE awards. Ogilvy PR integrates deeply with all Ogilvy & Mather disciplines (advertising, direct marketing, activation, promotional, digital and entertainment) through the proprietary Ogilvy Fusion™ approach to delivering comprehensive, business solutions through content creation, community building, and communications with measurable results.

 

The Ogilvy Public Relations network in China also includes H-Line and Ogilvy Commonhealth.

Debby Cheung

President, Ogilvy Public Relations China/Hong Kong
President, Ogilvy & Mather Shanghai

Debby’s expertise lies in corporate and consumer communications, as well as crisis management. Over the years, she has advised clients across a range of industries including luxury, food, tourism and retail. She has deep insight in company strategy and brand building. She joined Ogilvy PR, Hong Kong in 1988 as an associate and rose to Managing Director in 1998. After a two-year stint at the Mission Hills Group in 2001 as their Executive Director overseeing corporate affairs, sales and marketing, she rejoined O&M Shanghai in 2003. She was recognized with the SABRE Outstanding Achievement Award in 2015

CP Yang

Chief Operating Officer
Ogilvy Public Relations, Beijing

CP Yang joined Ogilvy Public Relations (Ogilvy PR) Beijing in 2006 after spending the previous 13 years with Ogilvy PR in Taiwan. As Chief Operating Officer of Ogilvy PR Beijing, CP works with the President of Ogilvy PR North Asia in managing corporate issues, knowledge management, training and major accounts. With more than 20 years of experience in issues and crisis management, multinational corporate communications, regional account counseling, and B2B communications, CP is also a brand team leader of one of Ogilvy & Mather Greater China’s biggest multinational accounts integrating public relations, direct marketing, and advertising expertise.

Selina Teng

President, Ogilvy & Mather Beijing
Co-Managing Director, Ogilvy Public Relations, Beijing

A native of Beijing, Selina joined Ogilvy Public Relations in 1999 and has been with Ogilvy & Mather ever since. Over the years, she has grown the IBM team into a sustainable technology practice for Ogilvy PR, pushing and developing it to keep Ogilvy ahead of the digital curve. Selina also helped build a highly successful, specialized automotive team, providing services to top international and local automotive brands since 2008. In addition to her role with Ogilvy PR, she also oversees the day-to-day business of the Ogilvy & Mather Group in Beijing with all Beijing units reporting to her.
