Job Opportunities

“If you always hire people who are smaller that you are, we shall become a company of dwarfs. If, on the other hand, you always hire people who are bigger than you are, we shall become a company of giants.”

 

– David Ogilvy

The Fellowship Project

The Fellowship Project is our annual intern recruitment program, where we look for the best and brightest university graduates and those in their last year of university.

 

Our Fellows get to work on a variety of projects and rotate across Ogilvy & Mather’s disciplines and departments to get first-hard work experience with our most talented creatives, planners, account managers, designers and technologists.
